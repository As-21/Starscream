About Starscream

Limitless ambition

Pride of the Cybertron War Academy, he's famous not only for his incredible skill, but also for his limitless (and troublesome) ambition

Transparent

Starscream's mutinous, power-grabbing plots are almost always transparent, and he can never muster up enough patience to complete a grandiose power play.

Destiny is inevitable

He never learns from his mistakes, despite his constant failures, Starscream believes it is only a matter of time before he prevails—his destiny is inevitable.
